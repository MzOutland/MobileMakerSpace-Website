What is a Mobile Makerspace?

A easy to create feature available for any teacher looking to increase learning. It will Provides mobility so that students can learn hands-on skills to create 3D printed items.

What are the limitations?

One of the main issues with incorporating a makerspace in classrooms is the lack of space. Often times faculty doesn't have a free room available, or the teacher is a "floating teacher".

Purpose of a Mobile MakerSpace

  • A convenient option to add 3D printing
  • Gives students the opportunity to explore
  • Specialty Role Training
  • Hands-on Programming
  • Add STEM to coursework
  • A way to collaborate
